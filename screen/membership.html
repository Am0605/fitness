How to Register

  1. Step 1: Select your desired membership package by reviewing the pricing plans above.
  2. Step 2: Proceed to our registration page and complete the online form.
  3. Step 3: For in-person registration, visit our centre during business hours and our friendly staff will guide you through the process.
  4. Step 4: Complete your payment online or at the reception with your preferred method.
  5. Step 5: You’ll receive a confirmation email or SMS with your membership details and access information.
